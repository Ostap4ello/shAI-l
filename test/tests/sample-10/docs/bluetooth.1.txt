NAME
     bluetooth - enable/disable internal bluetooth device

SYNOPSIS
     bluetooth [command]

COMMANDS
     on     Switch device on.

     off    Switch device off.

     toggle
            Toggle device state.

     cycle  Toggle the device twice (to return to the original state).

     <none>
            Show device state.

     --version
            Print TLP version.

SEE ALSO
     nfc(1), wifi(1), wwan(1), tlp(8).
